Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of bread 
 - 1/2 tablespoon butter
 
Directions:
 1. Prepare butter by removing it from the refrigerator and letting it sit in room temperature. 
 
 2. Toast bread in the toaster or oven.
 
 3. When bread is done toasting, remove from toaster/oven and use a (butter) knife to spread
    butter over bread.
